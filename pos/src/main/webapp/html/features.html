Features of This Application

This application is designed to make your life easier.

  • Record brands and categories for easy organization.
  • Keep track of your inventory and the products you have in stock.
  • Record previous orders and easily place new ones.
  • Update inventory automatically when orders are placed.
  • Generate reports based on brands, inventory, or sales data.
  • Create daily reports to track revenue and other important metrics.
  • Effortlessly monitor your business with incredible efficiency.
